# Windows.ApplicationModel.Email.DataProvider.EmailMailboxProposeNewTimeForMeetingRequest.ReportCompletedAsync

## -description
Informs the email client that the request was processed successfully.

Successful completion of the asynchronous operation indicates that the proposal was sent successfully.

## -returns
An asynchronous move operation on an [EmailMailboxProposeNewTimeForMeetingRequest](emailmailboxproposenewtimeformeetingrequest.md).
