# Windows.UI.Xaml.Controls.ComboBox.TextBoxStyle

## -description
Gets or sets the style of the TextBox in the ComboBox when the ComboBox is editable.



## -property-value

The style of the TextBox in the ComboBox when the ComboBox is editable.
