# Windows.Media.Audio.AudioNodeEmitterDecayModel.NaturalProperties

## -description
Gets the properties of a natural [AudioNodeEmitterDecayModel](audionodeemitterdecaymodel.md).

## -property-value
The properties of a natural decay model.

## -remarks
Set the properties of a natural decay model by calling [CreateNatural](audionodeemitterdecaymodel_createnatural.md).
