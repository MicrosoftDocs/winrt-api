# ContactEmailKind

## -description
Specifies the kinds of email addresses for a contact.

## -enum-fields
### -field Personal:0
The personal email address of the contact.

### -field Work:1
The work email address of the contact.

### -field Other:2
An email address of the contact other than personal or work.
