# Windows.Devices.Lights.LampInfo.Index

## -description
Zero based index of the lamp.  Every lamp is associated with one unique index.

## -property-value
The lamp index.

## -remarks
Valid indexes are in the range of 0 to [LampCount](lamparray_lampcount.md) - 1.
